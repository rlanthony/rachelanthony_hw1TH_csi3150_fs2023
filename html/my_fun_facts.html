Some Fun Facts about Me

Current Favorite Book: The Fourth Wing by Rebecca Yarros

Yarros' The Fourth Wing is my current favorite book because it hooks you in right from the first page and transports you to a fantasy world with a setting, plot, and characters you will not want to leave. The main character Violet challenges the expectations that have been imposed on her by society that ultimately will put her life in danger, causing her to build relationships with unlikely people in attempt to survive.
