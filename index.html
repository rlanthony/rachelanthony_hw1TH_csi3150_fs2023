About Me

Hello, my name is Rachel Anthony, and I am currently a senior at Oakland University studying management information systems and computer science. The technical and interpersonal skills I have developed in the classroom caused me to develop interests in the data analytics and data science fields. Since November 2022, I have worked at BorgWarner as an IT Network Mobility Intern supporting mobile phones for over 2,000 North American Employees. I have acquired skills in problem solving through troubleshooting the hardware and software of mobile devices in addition to managing billing and accounts for the services. This past summer, I was a Global IT Intern at General Motors where I automated one of their change management reports in the manufacturing environment to decrease the creation time by 95%. Ultimately, my educational and professional experiences spark an interest in developing my analytical skills potentially in the healthcare, government, or automtive industry.

In addition to my academics at Oakland University, I was involved in the Meadow Brook Ball Committee, serving as President and Tresurer during my involvement. This student organization plans the annual ball for 300 students using funds generated through fundraisers and student organization fundings from the university. During my time on the executive board, I acquired skills in budget management, contracts and negotiation, event planning, and managing teams of 10-20 individuals. All in all, my involvement in this committee allowed me to strengthen my communication and interpersonal skills to be effective in the corporate environment.
